## Introduction

Sushi Dai Toyosu is one of the best-known sushi destinations around the Toyosu Market area. It is popular for ultra-fresh seafood, precise Edomae technique, and a counter experience that starts early in the day.

## Why Visit

- Morning omakase flow with strong seasonal focus
- Excellent tuna cuts and shellfish quality
- Convenient for travelers combining Toyosu and Odaiba plans

## Practical Tips

- Visit early because lines can build quickly.
- Keep your schedule flexible for waiting time.
- If you prefer a quieter meal, target non-peak weekdays.
